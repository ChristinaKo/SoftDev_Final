How do I use it?
With FoodMe! you can search for a recipe by its name or by ingredients. You can also create a user account to add a specific recipe to your favorites.
Do I have to create an account?
Creating a user account is not necessary. All search features are available to nonusers, but those who want to save recipes to a favorites list must create an account.
What does the Random tab do?:
Clicking the Random button will, as its name suggests, return a different random recipe every time it is clicked.
What does "Add To Favorites" mean?:
When the "Add to Favorites" button is clicked (and you are logged in!), the current recipe will be added to your Favorites tab!
